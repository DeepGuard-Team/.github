# DeepGuard

**Video verification infrastructure for newsrooms and digital publishers.**

The fake doesn't have to be convincing. It just has to move faster than the people trying to check it.

DeepGuard gives journalists and editors a timestamped, citable, exportable evidence package — not a confidence score. When a manipulated video surfaces during a breaking news cycle, there's no time to debate what the AI thinks. There's only time to act and document that you acted responsibly.

---

## What we do

Upload a video URL or file. Get back a PDF evidence package: manipulation scoring across sub-signals, audio-visual sync analysis, metadata extraction, and live cross-referenced sources — ready to attach to an editorial decision, a legal file, or a regulator's inquiry.

Others flag. We prove.

---

## Who we build for

Breaking news editors, digital publishers, and fact-checking organizations operating under the EU AI Act, DSA, and the growing weight of content liability. The cost of publishing a manipulated video isn't just reputational — it's up to €20M in regulatory fines and €10K–€50K per churned enterprise client.

DeepGuard costs less than a single retraction.
